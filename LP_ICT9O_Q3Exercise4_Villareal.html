Tokyo


DESCRIPTION:

Tokyo is Japan's capital and the world's most populous metropolis. Tokyo provides both tradition and innovation, as well as countless options for dining, shopping, and sightseeing. Tokyo provides visitors with what seems like an endless variety of dining, entertainment, shopping, and cultural opportunities. There are a wide variety of top-notch museums, ancient temples, and gardens that allow visitors to fully experience the city's heritage.

MY EXPERIENCE:

I went to Tokyo last 2017 as a reward for having finished the school year with an "Honors" title. I was really looking forward to it, having heard great things about it from my friends. And let me tell you this: It was as amazing and magical as what I've been told. The surroundings were so clean and quiet. It also felt like a safe place for anyone to stroll around, no matter what place or time. Tokyo also offers a great number of tourist attractions, such as Tokyo Disneyland, Tokyo Disney Sea, Tokyo Skytree, Mount Fuji, and many more. A personal favorite of mine is Tokyo Disney Sea; it stood out among the previous Disney parks I've been to. Overall, I enjoyed my entire 8 days in Tokyo. I absolutely adore the city and cannot wait to go back soon.
